## RTM Purpose

The Requirements Traceability Matrix ensures:

### 1. Requirement Coverage

Every functional requirement is implemented within the dashboard.

### 2. Testing Coverage

Each requirement is validated through UAT scenarios.

### 3. Stakeholder Alignment

Business needs remain mapped to user stories.

### 4. Change Tracking

Requirement changes can be traced to affected visuals and reports.
