# SQL Analysis Insights

## Q1: Overall Churn Rate
- Total Customers: 7,043
- Churned Customers: 1,869
- Churn Rate: 26.54%
- Insight: More than one-fourth of customers have churned, indicating a significant retention challenge.

## Q2: Churn Rate by Contract Type
- Month-to-Month customers have the highest churn rate.
- Long-term contracts significantly reduce customer churn.
- Insight: Contract commitment strongly influences retention.

## Q3: Churn Rate by Internet Service
- Fiber Optic customers show the highest churn rate.
- DSL customers have moderate churn.
- Customers without internet service show the lowest churn.
- Insight: Fiber service experience may require improvement.

## Q4: Churn Rate by Payment Method
- Electronic Check users have the highest churn.
- Automatic payment users show better retention.
- Insight: Encouraging automatic payments may improve customer loyalty.

## Q5: Churn Rate by Tenure Group
- Customers in the first 0–12 months have the highest churn risk.
- Churn decreases as tenure increases.
- Insight: Early customer engagement is critical.

## Q6: Churn Rate by Tech Support Status
- Customers without Tech Support churn significantly more.
- Tech Support subscribers show lower churn.
- Insight: Support services directly impact retention.

## Q7: Average Monthly Charges
- Churned customers generally pay higher monthly charges.
- Retained customers have lower average monthly costs.
- Insight: Pricing sensitivity contributes to churn behavior.

## Q8: Revenue at Risk Analysis
- High-value churned customers contribute significantly to revenue loss.
- Revenue at Risk exceeds $139K.
- Insight: Retaining high-value customers should be a business priority.

## Q9: Churn Rate by Senior Citizen Status
- Senior citizens show higher churn rates compared to non-senior customers.
- Insight: Specialized retention strategies may be needed for senior customers.

## Q10: Multi-Factor Risk Analysis
- Highest churn risk observed among:
  - Month-to-Month Contract
  - Fiber Optic Internet
  - No Tech Support
- Insight: Customers with multiple risk factors require targeted intervention.
