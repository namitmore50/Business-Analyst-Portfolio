# Business Problem

## Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most critical workforce challenges affecting organizational stability, productivity, and long-term business performance. High employee turnover increases recruitment costs, training expenses, operational disruptions, and knowledge loss.

A global workforce organization operating across multiple departments has experienced rising attrition levels, particularly in employee-intensive functions such as Sales, Technology, Operations, and Research & Development.

Leadership teams currently lack a centralized analytics system to understand workforce behavior, monitor attrition risk, and make proactive workforce planning decisions.

---

## Business Problem Statement

The organization is facing multiple workforce management challenges:

### 1. Rising Employee Attrition
- Increasing employee exits are affecting workforce continuity.
- Early-tenure employees show higher resignation tendencies.
- Overtime and work-life imbalance are contributing to employee dissatisfaction.

### 2. Workforce Planning Challenges
- Departments face uneven workforce performance and hiring demand.
- Leadership lacks visibility into hiring funnel effectiveness and workforce gaps.

### 3. Employee Satisfaction & Retention Issues
- Lower job satisfaction is linked with higher attrition risk.
- Delayed promotions and compensation concerns negatively impact employee retention.

### 4. Financial Impact of Attrition
- Employee turnover significantly increases:
  - Hiring costs
  - Training expenses
  - Productivity loss
  - Workforce replacement costs

Statistical analysis estimated an average attrition replacement cost of approximately **$62.7K per employee exit**.

---

## Key Business Questions

Leadership requires answers to the following questions:

1. Why are employees leaving the organization?
2. Which departments are at the highest attrition risk?
3. What workforce factors influence employee exits?
4. How does overtime affect employee retention?
5. Which employee segments are most vulnerable to attrition?
6. What is the financial cost of employee turnover?
7. How effective is the current hiring funnel?
8. How can workforce planning be improved?

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to build an **end-to-end HR Intelligence & Workforce Analytics Command Center** that enables business leaders to:

- Monitor workforce attrition trends
- Identify root causes of employee exits
- Detect high-risk employee segments
- Forecast workforce demand
- Evaluate recruitment effectiveness
- Measure the financial cost of attrition
- Benchmark salary competitiveness
- Improve workforce planning decisions
- Prioritize employee retention initiatives
