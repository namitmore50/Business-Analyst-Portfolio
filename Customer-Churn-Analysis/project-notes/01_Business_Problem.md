# Business Problem Statement

## Project Title

Customer Churn & Retention Strategy Dashboard

## Business Context

A telecom company is experiencing increasing customer churn, leading to recurring revenue loss and reduced customer lifetime value.

Management wants to understand:

1. Why customers are leaving
2. Which customer segments are at highest churn risk
3. Which services, pricing models, or customer behaviors influence churn
4. What actions can reduce churn and improve customer retention

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to identify churn drivers, quantify revenue risk, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Expected Business Impact

* Reduced customer churn
* Better retention strategy
* Higher recurring revenue
* Improved customer lifetime value
