# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Project Title

**Global Workforce Attrition & HR Intelligence Analytics**

---

## Document Purpose

This Business Requirements Document (BRD) defines the business problem, project objectives, stakeholder expectations, scope, requirements, and success criteria for the **Global Workforce Attrition & HR Intelligence Analytics** solution.

The document serves as a foundation for building an integrated workforce intelligence dashboard that enables data-driven HR and workforce planning decisions.

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Employee attrition significantly impacts workforce productivity, operational continuity, employee morale, and organizational cost efficiency.

The organization currently lacks a centralized analytics platform to monitor employee attrition trends, identify workforce risks, analyze hiring effectiveness, and estimate attrition-related financial impact.

This project aims to build a centralized **HR Intelligence & Workforce Analytics Command Center** to support workforce retention, recruitment optimization, workforce planning, and executive decision-making.

---

# 2. Business Problem Statement

The organization faces multiple workforce management challenges:

### Workforce Attrition
- Increasing employee exits across departments
- Limited visibility into attrition drivers
- High early-stage employee turnover

### Workforce Planning Challenges
- Difficulty forecasting workforce demand
- Uneven staffing and performance across departments
- Limited hiring funnel visibility

### Employee Satisfaction Challenges
- Work-life balance concerns
- Overtime-related burnout
- Lower workforce engagement

### Financial Challenges
- High recruitment and onboarding costs
- Productivity loss from employee exits
- Lack of visibility into attrition-related expenses

Leadership currently struggles to:

- Identify high-risk workforce segments
- Understand employee exit drivers
- Measure cost of attrition
- Monitor recruitment effectiveness
- Benchmark workforce performance
- Prioritize retention initiatives

---

# 3. Business Objectives

The project aims to enable leadership to:

1. Monitor workforce attrition trends
2. Identify root causes of employee exits
3. Detect high-risk employee groups
4. Improve workforce planning
5. Measure recruitment effectiveness
6. Quantify attrition-related costs
7. Benchmark salary competitiveness
8. Improve workforce retention strategy
9. Support executive workforce decision-making

---

# 4. Project Scope

## In Scope

### Workforce Analytics
- Workforce attrition analysis
- Department-level risk assessment
- Employee satisfaction analysis
- Tenure-based attrition analysis
- Overtime risk analysis

### Workforce Planning
- Hiring funnel analysis
- Workforce performance monitoring
- KPI achievement tracking
- Workforce productivity segmentation

### Financial Analysis
- Cost of attrition estimation
- Salary benchmark comparison
- Department cost exposure analysis

### Business Intelligence
- Executive KPI dashboard
- Workforce storytelling
- Interactive drill-down reporting
- Business recommendations

### Technical Analytics
- SQL analysis
- Statistical testing
- Correlation analysis
- Workforce risk segmentation

---

## Out of Scope

The following capabilities are excluded from this project:

- Payroll processing
- Attendance management
- Employee leave management
- Real-time HRMS integration
- Predictive machine learning deployment
- Employee-level confidential HR intervention systems

# 5. Functional Requirements

| Requirement ID | Requirement | Priority |
|----------------|-------------|----------|
| FR-01 | Dashboard must display workforce attrition KPIs | High |
| FR-02 | Users must analyze attrition by department | High |
| FR-03 | Dashboard must identify high-risk employee segments | High |
| FR-04 | Users must view workforce planning metrics | High |
| FR-05 | Dashboard must estimate cost of attrition | High |
| FR-06 | Users must compare internal salary vs market benchmark | Medium |
| FR-07 | Dashboard must support executive drill-down analysis | Medium |
| FR-08 | Dashboard must provide workforce recommendations | Medium |
