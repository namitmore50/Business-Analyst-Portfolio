# 13_Executive_Recommendations

# Executive Recommendations

The following recommendations were developed based on SQL analysis, Python EDA, RFM segmentation, SLA monitoring, profitability analysis, and Power BI dashboards.

---

## REC-01 — Launch RFM Re-Engagement Campaign

### Finding

The analysis identified approximately **23,246 At-Risk customers**, representing a major customer churn opportunity.

### Impact

Losing these customers could significantly reduce repeat purchase revenue and long-term customer lifetime value.

### Recommended Action

Launch targeted retention campaigns including:

* Personalized discount offers
* Email remarketing
* Loyalty incentives
* Re-engagement bundles

Focus specifically on **At-Risk** and **Needs Attention** customer segments.

### Estimated ROI

Assuming only **10% reactivation** of At-Risk customers:

**Estimated Revenue Recovery: ~R$4.77M**

### Priority

**High**

---

## REC-02 — Strengthen Seller SLA Compliance

### Finding

Seller tier analysis showed that **At-Risk sellers significantly underperform Gold-tier sellers** in delivery compliance.

Late deliveries negatively influence customer reviews and operational satisfaction.

### Impact

Poor SLA performance increases:

* Negative reviews
* Complaint rates
* Customer churn risk

### Recommended Action

Implement:

* Minimum seller SLA threshold (**80% compliance**)
* Seller performance monitoring
* Logistics escalation workflows
* Delisting for consistently poor performers

### Estimated ROI

A **5–10% improvement in SLA performance** could significantly reduce late deliveries and improve customer satisfaction scores.

### Priority

**High**

---

## REC-03 — Expand High-Performing Product Categories

### Finding

Categories including:

* **bed_bath_table**
* **health_beauty**
* **computers_accessories**

consistently outperformed other categories in revenue and order volume.

### Impact

Increasing investment in high-performing categories can accelerate revenue growth.

### Recommended Action

Increase:

* Inventory allocation
* Marketing investment
* Promotional campaigns
* Seller partnerships

for high-performing categories.

### Estimated ROI

Expected revenue uplift through optimized category investment:

**~8–12% revenue growth**

### Priority

**Medium**

## REC-04 — Reduce Complaint Rate in Low-Review Categories

### Finding

Certain categories showed elevated complaint rates and lower review scores.

### Impact

Poor customer satisfaction negatively impacts retention and brand trust.

### Recommended Action

Introduce:

* Product quality audits
* Seller quality monitoring
* Automated complaint escalation

for low-performing categories.

### Estimated ROI

Reduction in complaints may improve customer satisfaction by **10–15%**.

### Priority

**Medium**
