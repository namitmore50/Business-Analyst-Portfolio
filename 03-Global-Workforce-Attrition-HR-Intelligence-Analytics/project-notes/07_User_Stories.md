# User Stories

## User Story Overview

The following user stories define stakeholder expectations and business requirements for the **Global Workforce Attrition & HR Intelligence Analytics Dashboard**.

Each story follows the Agile user story format:

> **As a [Role], I want [Goal], so that [Business Value].**

---

| ID | Persona | User Story | Priority | Status |
|----|----------|-------------|----------|--------|
| US-01 | CHRO | As a CHRO, I want to identify departments with the highest attrition so that I can prioritize workforce retention strategies. | High | Completed |
| US-02 | HR Manager | As an HR Manager, I want to identify high-risk employee segments so that proactive retention interventions can be implemented. | High | Completed |
| US-03 | Recruitment Team | As a recruiter, I want to analyze hiring funnel effectiveness so that recruitment bottlenecks and delays can be reduced. | High | Completed |
| US-04 | Finance Team | As a finance stakeholder, I want to estimate employee attrition cost so that workforce-related financial losses can be quantified. | High | Completed |
| US-05 | Department Head | As a department manager, I want to compare workforce performance and attrition across teams so that corrective actions can be prioritized. | Medium | Completed |
| US-06 | Executive Leadership | As executive leadership, I want an executive workforce dashboard so that organizational workforce health can be monitored quickly. | High | Completed |
| US-07 | HR Business Partner (HRBP) | As an HRBP, I want salary benchmarking insights so that compensation competitiveness gaps can be identified. | Medium | Completed |
| US-08 | Operations Leadership | As operations leadership, I want workforce planning visibility so that staffing shortages and operational risks can be reduced. | Medium | Completed |

---

# Acceptance Criteria

## US-01 — Department Attrition Analysis
### Acceptance Criteria
- User can view department-wise attrition
- High-risk departments are visually highlighted
- Attrition trends are filterable

---

## US-02 — High-Risk Employee Identification
### Acceptance Criteria
- Risk categories are visible
- High-risk workforce segments are identifiable
- HR intervention areas are highlighted

---

## US-03 — Recruitment Funnel Analysis
### Acceptance Criteria
- Hiring stages are visible
- Conversion rates are measurable
- Funnel bottlenecks can be identified

---

## US-04 — Attrition Cost Estimation
### Acceptance Criteria
- Attrition cost is visible
- Department cost exposure is measurable
- Financial impact is quantified

---

## US-05 — Workforce Performance Comparison
### Acceptance Criteria
- Team performance is comparable
- KPI achievement trends are visible
- Workforce stability indicators are available

---

## US-06 — Executive Dashboard Visibility
### Acceptance Criteria
- Executive KPIs are displayed
- Workforce health is summarized
- Business risks are identifiable

---

## US-07 — Salary Benchmarking
### Acceptance Criteria
- Salary benchmark comparisons are visible
- Compensation gaps are identifiable
- Market positioning is measurable

---

## US-08 — Workforce Planning Visibility
### Acceptance Criteria
- Hiring funnel is accessible
- Workforce capability metrics are visible
- Staffing risks are identifiable

---

# Final Outcome

These user stories ensure the dashboard aligns with stakeholder expectations and business objectives while supporting **data-driven workforce decision-making**.
