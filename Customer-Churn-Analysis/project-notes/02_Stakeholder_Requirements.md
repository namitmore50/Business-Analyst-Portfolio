# Stakeholder Requirements

## CEO / Executive Management

### Key Questions

* What is the overall churn rate?
* How much revenue is being lost due to churn?
* Which customer segments are most at risk?

### Required KPIs

* Total Customers
* Customers Churned
* Churn Rate %
* Revenue at Risk
* Retention Rate %

---

## Customer Success Team

### Key Questions

* Which customers are leaving most frequently?
* Which customer groups require retention strategies?
* Which services influence churn?

### Required Insights

* Churn by Contract Type
* Churn by Tenure
* Churn by Internet Service
* Churn by Tech Support

---

## Finance Team

### Key Questions

* What revenue is being lost from churned customers?
* Which pricing patterns affect churn?

### Required KPIs

* Monthly Revenue at Risk
* Monthly Charges vs Churn
* High-Risk Revenue Segments

---

## Product / Service Team

### Key Questions

* Which services are linked to higher churn?
* Does lack of tech support influence churn?

### Required Insights

* Internet Service Type Analysis
* Tech Support Impact
* Online Security & Backup Analysis

---

## Marketing Team

### Key Questions

* Which customer segments should receive offers?
* Which customer groups are most loyal?

### Required Insights

* Churn by Senior Citizen
* Churn by Partner / Dependents
* High-Risk Customer Segments
