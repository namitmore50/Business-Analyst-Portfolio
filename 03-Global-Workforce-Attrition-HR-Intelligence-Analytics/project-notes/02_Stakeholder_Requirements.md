# Stakeholder Requirements

## Stakeholder Analysis

This project involves multiple business stakeholders across Human Resources, Workforce Planning, Finance, Recruitment, and Executive Leadership. Each stakeholder group has distinct reporting needs, business concerns, and decision-making priorities.

The dashboard is designed to provide role-specific insights that support workforce stability, retention, hiring efficiency, and cost optimization.

---

## Primary Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Role | Business Objective | Key Requirement | Dashboard Focus |
|-------------|------|-------------------|-----------------|----------------|
| CHRO | HR Leadership | Improve workforce retention | Identify attrition drivers and workforce risks | Executive Overview, Attrition Deep Dive |
| HR Managers | Workforce Operations | Monitor workforce stability | Detect high-risk departments and employee segments | Attrition Analytics, Risk Matrix |
| Recruitment Team | Talent Acquisition | Improve hiring effectiveness | Analyze hiring funnel performance and recruitment quality | Workforce Planning |
| Department Heads | Operations Management | Improve team retention | Monitor department workforce health | Attrition Deep Dive |
| Finance Team | Cost Management | Reduce attrition-related costs | Measure cost impact of employee turnover | Cost of Attrition |
| Executive Leadership | Strategic Decision-Making | Enable data-driven workforce decisions | View centralized workforce intelligence | Executive Dashboard |

---

## Stakeholder Business Questions

### 1. CHRO (Chief Human Resources Officer)

#### Business Questions
- Which departments have the highest attrition risk?
- What are the major causes of employee exits?
- Which workforce segments require retention intervention?
- How does employee satisfaction impact attrition?

#### Success Metrics
- Reduced employee turnover
- Improved employee retention
- Increased workforce satisfaction

---

### 2. HR Managers

#### Business Questions
- Which employees are at high attrition risk?
- How does overtime impact employee exits?
- Which departments require immediate HR intervention?
- What workforce trends are emerging?

#### Success Metrics
- Reduced high-risk attrition
- Better workforce monitoring
- Faster intervention planning

---

### 3. Recruitment Team

#### Business Questions
- Which recruitment channels perform best?
- What are the hiring funnel drop-off points?
- Which channels produce high-performing employees?
- Where do hiring bottlenecks occur?

#### Success Metrics
- Improved hiring conversion
- Better recruitment efficiency
- Faster hiring cycle

---

### 4. Department Heads

#### Business Questions
- Which teams have workforce instability?
- How does employee satisfaction vary by department?
- Which teams are most vulnerable to attrition?
- What actions can reduce employee exits?

#### Success Metrics
- Improved department retention
- Better workforce productivity
- Stable team performance

---

### 5. Finance Team

#### Business Questions
- What is the financial cost of attrition?
- How much does employee replacement cost?
- Which departments create the highest cost exposure?
- What cost savings are possible through retention?

#### Success Metrics
- Reduced workforce replacement costs
- Better workforce cost visibility
- Improved HR investment decisions

---

### 6. Executive Leadership

#### Business Questions
- What are the biggest workforce risks?
- How healthy is the workforce overall?
- Which areas require strategic investment?
- How can attrition be reduced at scale?

#### Success Metrics
- Better workforce planning
- Improved executive decision-making
- Reduced organizational risk

## Dashboard Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if stakeholders can:

- Identify high-risk employee segments
- Monitor workforce KPIs effectively
- Understand root causes of attrition
- Improve hiring funnel performance
- Quantify attrition-related costs
- Make proactive workforce decisions
- Improve employee retention strategy
- Reduce avoidable workforce risk

---

## Expected Business Outcome

The dashboard should enable leadership to transition from **reactive workforce management** to **proactive workforce intelligence**, improving retention, operational stability, and workforce planning efficiency.
